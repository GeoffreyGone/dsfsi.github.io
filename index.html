Welcome

Welcome to the Data Science for Social Impact Research group based at the University of Pretoria, South Africa. The group is led by Dr. Vukosi Marivate, who is the ABSA Chair of Data Science. This website keeps track of work the group working on.

The group looks at the following areas:

- Machine Learning [ML]
- Natural Language Processing [NLP]
- Social Media [SM]
- Society [Soc]
- Web Technologies [WT]
